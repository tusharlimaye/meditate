MEDITATION EXERCISE

WITH MEDITATION COMES PHYSICAL AND MENTAL BENEFITS. YOU WILL BECOME THE EMBODIMENT OF PEACE AND CHARGED WITH A NEW FOUND ENERGY. YOUR MIND WILL BE SHARPEND AND YOU WILL BE ABLE TO CUT THE TAPE THAT IS BLOCKING YOU FROM YOUR TRUE POTENTIAL.


-SIT IN A CHAIR THAT CAN HELP YOU ACHIEVE GOOD POSTURE AND PLACE YOUR PALMS ON YOUR LAP.


-YOU CAN DO THIS IN THE DAY OR AT NIGHT, WITH CANDLES OR IN COMPLETE DARKNESS.


-JUST SIT FOR A FEW MINUTES AND RELAX, &THEN VISUALIZE A BRIGHT GREEN LIGHT. IMAGINE THIS LIGHT SURROUNDING YOU AND ENTERING YOUR BODY, AND BECOMING ONE WITH THE GREEN LIGHT.


-FEEL THE POSITIVE ENERGY. YOU CAN START OUT FOR ABOUT TEN MINUTES AND GRADUALLY INCREASE THE TIME UNTIL YOU REACH TWENTY-FIVE MINUTES, OR ANY LENGTH OF TIME YOU FEEL COMFORTABLE DOING.
